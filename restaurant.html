RESTAURANT

FOOD AND DRINK


We know that food and drink is the road to many of our guests hearts. From middle eastern feats, Italian classics and sourdough pizzas in our garden courtyard, we offer a little bit of everything and are a neighbourhood favourite. Right in the heart of Copenhagen.

RESTAURANT & BAR

Located in the heart of the hotel, our restaurant and bar offers a variety of Italian classics paired with exclusive wines and cocktails.

RESTAURANT DADA

Dada is a personal interpretation of the Middle Eastern kitchen stretching along the Mediterranean coast focusing on sharing dishes. Indulge in grilled meats, greens, spicy fish, colourful salads, grilled breads and a world of mezze and dips.

CHRISTMAS & NEW YEAR

This year our restaurants remain closed on Christmas Eve, but it will be possible to order a special Christmas menu as room service on the 24th & 25th of December. If you are looking for a place to celebrate New Year's Eve, our Middle Eastern inspired restaurant Dada is ready to celebrate with a blast.
